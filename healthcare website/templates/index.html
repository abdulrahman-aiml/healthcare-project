In the early years (2008-2014), CareCloud began as a concept to integrate healthcare solutions into one seamless platform. Our team started researching innovative ways to provide healthcare services through technology, focusing on user-centric designs and accessibility.

From 2014 to 2016, CareCloud's development focused on adding key features such as the chatbot assistant and improving the user experience. During this phase, we also partnered with healthcare providers to integrate online medical stores, offering users easy access to healthcare products.

Between 2016 and 2019, CareCloud expanded its offerings, introducing the ability to book and reschedule appointments seamlessly. This marked a major milestone as we implemented AI-driven features to improve the platformâ€™s usability, efficiency, and patient engagement.

From 2019 onwards, CareCloud evolved into a fully integrated healthcare platform, with constant updates, new features, and continuous user feedback. The platform now provides a comprehensive suite of services, including 24/7 support, a wide range of healthcare products, and reliable appointment management.
